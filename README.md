# Lucineer

> Structured lucid dream practice tool.

**[lucineer.com](https://lucineer.com)**

Lucineer teaches lucid dreaming as a skill, not a gift. Techniques backed by sleep research, tracked with real data, adapted to what works for you. Not a dream dictionary, not a crystal shop.

## What It Does

- **Technique guides** — Reality Testing (RCT), WBTB, SSILD with evidence citations and step-by-step instructions
- **Dream sign tracking** — Automatically surfaces recurring dream themes and their frequency
- **Progress charts** — Monthly lucid rate, reality check compliance, improvement over time
- **Practice history** — See what techniques you've used and what actually works for you

## Core Techniques

| Technique | Difficulty | Evidence |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Reality Testing (RCT) | Easy | LaBerge & Rheingold, 1990 |
| WBTB (Wake Back to Bed) | Medium | Stumbrys et al., 2012 |
| SSILD (Senses Initiated) | Beginner-friendly | Community-validated |
